## What to build

Cap each astronaut stat (strength, agility, endurance, intelligence, perception, luck) at 30. Once a stat reaches 30, training recipes that would increase it have no effect (or are not offered / shown as unavailable).

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Each of the 6 stats has a hard cap of 30
- [ ] Training a stat that is already at 30 does not increase it further
- [ ] The player receives some indication that a stat is maxed (e.g. the recipe does nothing, or the UI shows it as capped)
- [ ] Stats below 30 still train normally
- [ ] The cap applies to all astronauts, including clones
